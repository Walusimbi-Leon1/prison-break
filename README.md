## About

This book is a comprehensive exploration of the *Prison Break* universe — the show that captivated millions worldwide with its story of a genius engineer who tattoos a prison blueprint on his body to break his wrongly condemned brother out of death row.

### What's Inside

- **15 chapters** covering every aspect of the series
- **20,000+ words** of analysis, recap, and appreciation
- **All 5 seasons** — from Fox River to Sona to Resurrection
- **Character deep-dives** — Michael, Lincoln, T-Bag, Sucre, Mahone, Sara, and more
- **Behind the scenes** — production details, filming locations, and actor chemistry
- **Funny moments** — humor in unexpected places
- **Cultural impact** — how the show changed television
